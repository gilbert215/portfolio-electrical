Deep Reinforcement Learning

Microgrid Energy Management with PPO

A DRL agent that learns to optimally dispatch energy in a solar-battery-grid microgrid using real hourly irradiance data from Kigali, Rwanda fetched directly from the NASA POWER API. The agent controls battery charge and discharge decisions every hour to minimise electricity costs and maximise solar utilisation.

PPO agent achieved +0.901 mean reward vs -5.209 for rule-based — generating revenue by selling surplus solar energy back to the grid.

Transformer · Time-Series

PMU Fault Detection & Classification

A three-stage deep learning pipeline that detects and classifies power grid faults from Phasor Measurement Unit (PMU) time-series data using a custom Transformer architecture. Trained on synthetic data and validated on a real PMU dataset from Virginia Tech published on Zenodo.

100% classification accuracy across 6 fault types: Normal, LG, LL, LLG, LLL, LLLG
Python PyTorch Transformer SHAP PMU Data Scikit-learn

Research Experience

June 2025 — August 2025 · Carnegie Mellon University Africa

Biogas System Implementation — Renewable Energy Impact Assessment

Led mixed-methods impact assessment for a productive use of renewable energy (PURE) project targeting 15,000 households across two districts in Rwanda. Conducted stakeholder interviews, cleaned and analysed multi-source datasets, and produced financial projections showing NPV of $2.6M and IRR of 4.8% over 20 years — demonstrating biogas as a sustainable alternative to firewood.

Dec 2022 — May 2023 · University of Rwanda & Heilbronn University

Solar Electrification — MURIKA Project

Conducted market analysis and stakeholder assessment to evaluate solar energy opportunities for 595,400 unelectrified households in Rwanda. Designed scalable plug-and-play solar systems ranging from EUR 165–225 per household. Implemented in Nyamagabe and Nyaruguru districts aligned with Rwanda's national energy goals.
